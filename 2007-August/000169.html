the missing files, put it a folder and add a section like this(without
the cuts):

        <repository type="rpm-dir" status="replaceable">
                <source path="/home/cyberorg/kiwiextra"/>
        </repository>
----cut end-----

/srv/tftpboot would be created.

kiwi-packages.

wiki page and the one on the top about the schemeversion. It is
simpler to set up if you do not modify anything else.
