- added ability to download images over ftp/http/https to netboot
linuxrc.  Requires curl.  The
  'kiwitftpserver' kernel param is renamed to 'kiwiserver'.  Set the
  'kiwiservertype' kernel param to one of 'tftp', 'ftp', 'http', or
'https' to download using the
  appropriate method.
