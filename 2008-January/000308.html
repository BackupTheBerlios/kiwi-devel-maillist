If you want to play with it you can enable NFSROOT instead of NBDROOT
and work with /srv/kiwi-ltsp-nfs chrooted. That way your changes will
be applied immediately and you would not have to rebuild the image.
Also explore all the switches to kiwi-ltsp-setup, they will save you a
lot of time when experimenting. Patches are welcome :)

Captain_mGNUs and I who have worked on the project and the lead KIWI
developer Schaefi are usually available on IRC #kiwi if you need any
quick help.
