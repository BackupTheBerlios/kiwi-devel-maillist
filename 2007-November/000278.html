The nbd config /etc/nbd-server/config generated by kiwi-ltsp-setup -s
has the wrong path to the squashfs image.
instead of

[kiwi-ltsp]
        exportname = /srv/kiwi-ltsp/ltsp-suse-10.3.x86_64-0.0.2

        exportname = /srv/kiwi-ltsp/ltsp-suse-10.3.i686-0.0.2

report? I went to the bugzilla, but there is only one bug ;-)
