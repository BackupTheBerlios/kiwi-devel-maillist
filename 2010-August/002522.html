Hi,

you may or may not know that the openSUSE Project has done a restart of there 
wiki with the goal  to provide high quality documentation and a place for 
collaboration on all parts of the project. 

This is done in a well structured, standardized and easy readable layout.
The Navigation happens through means common to any wiki, but there are also 
portals. Portals are entry points for a specific topic, similar to the main 
page.

So please have a look at our new KIWI Portal page at

	http://en.opensuse.org/Portal:KIWI
