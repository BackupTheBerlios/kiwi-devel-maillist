Attached is a patch that adds __validateConsistency as a "private"
helper to provide a place to add consistency checks. The initial
consistency check __checkPostDumpAction ensures that only one of

oem-bootwait, oem-reboot, oem-reboot-interactive, oem-shutdown,
oem-shutdown-interactive

is set to true in the config.xml file.
