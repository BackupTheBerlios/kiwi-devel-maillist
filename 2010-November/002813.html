Hi,

The attached patch moves two consistency checks (kiwirevision and
version format) out of the KIWIXML constructor and into their own
methods. Each method is called from the recently added
__validateConsistency(). In addition the patch implements a new
consistency check, __checkFilesysSpec(). This method assures that the
"filesystem" attribute is used for image types that require the attribute.
