Hello Andreas,

> Have no read anything why you called it Kiwi in first place? Can you
> explain? BTW "Kiwi" denominates actually a flightless bird and a
> nickname for New Zealanders. "Kiwi" as a fruit is  called Kiwi fruit
> down here and not KIWI. So when you say fruit lover, that is from a New
> Zealand perspective (where the term originated) not entirely correct ;)

*hui* I was just waiting for that question :) Ok, it starts at home
when I was hacking the first version of what you know as kiwi. At that
time it didn't had a name and my wife was asking why I'm still working
on something... I said it's not something it could be cool stuff but
I didn't have a name for it. So I asked her what do you think ? Well
she was reading in a nature magazine (nabu) and because she didn't
know as well and I think she wasn't really interested in all that
boring programming stuff :) she just picked up the name of the bird
the magazin reports about. So the name "kiwi" was born. As you may know
the kiwi needs to be protected there aren't much of them and because
this destiny applied to a former project with a similar background I
kept the name.

But as the user base for kiwi grows almost all people didn't thought
of the bird but of the fruit. I wasn't aware that the fruit is named
"Kiwi fruit" thanks for explaining it to me. I think it was Guy Lunardi
who first used the notion of the "Fruit lovers" and I thought *wow*
that's a perfect name for the image building comunity. It's sweet, healthy
and tastes good :-)) I like it

I'm hoping the New Zealanders will forgive me when I didn't used
the term entirely correctly ;)
