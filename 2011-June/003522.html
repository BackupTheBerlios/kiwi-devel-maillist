Hi,

Just stumbled across this. The patch removes the call to sax2 in the 
11.3 vm guest example, sax2 is no longer part of 11.3 and later as the 
X-server is self configuring. Also added code to allow the firstboot 
sequence to be initiated. While this particular example does not use the 
firstboot mechanism, someone basing their build of the example and 
trying to get firstboot to work would be hard pressed to find why it is 
not working.
