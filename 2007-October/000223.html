Hi

The attached patch allows creation of leaner netboot image suitable
for booting diskless clients.

It does not have filesystem tools and various other drivers that are
normally in default and xen images.

kiwi --prepare /usr/share/kiwi/image/netboot/suse-10.3 --root /tmp/kiwi-tmp  \
--add-profile xen

kiwi --prepare /usr/share/kiwi/image/netboot/suse-10.3 --root /tmp/kiwi-tmp  \
--add-profile diskless

Without --add-profle the default image will be built.
