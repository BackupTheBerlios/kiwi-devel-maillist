Here's a patch which adds a 'realname' attribute to the 'user' element,
which lets you specify the user's full name (e.g., John Doe).  It also
fixes a small issue in pxeboot where the rw partition is assumed to be
the third partition of the disk.  The patch causes the rw partition to
be inferred from the position of the ro one.
