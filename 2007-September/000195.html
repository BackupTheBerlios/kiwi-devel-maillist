Hi

Here is the patch to kiwi netboot that enables nbd swap if a thin
client has less than 48M RAM available.

Schaefi, you would have to take a good look at it if it would work.

I have added two more options to the config.<mac/default>, swap port
and swap device with its defaults if nothing is specified in config.

NBDROOT=<serverip>;<nbdrootPort>;<nbdrootdev\/dev/nb0>;<nbdswapPort/9210>

I am told that there are a lot of thin clients with 32 M ram out there :)
